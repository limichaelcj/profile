About

Chinese-American born and raised in Wisconsin. I graduated with a degree in Biology, but after college decided to travel. I taught English in Taiwan for a few years and eventually decided to pursue my interest in programming. My only experience in programming is through my own self-study and exploration. I love to create both practical and artistic works and hope to use what we learn at Le Wagon to make something amazing for people to use and enjoy.
